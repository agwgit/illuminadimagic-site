Meet Illuminadi

Before embracing his life as a magician, Illuminadi pursued a career as a pharmacist. This unique background allows him to blend the art of magic with the science behind it, creating a one-of-a-kind experience. As a magician member of the esteemed Academy of Magical Arts and a performer at renowned venues, including The World Famous Magic Castle and House of Cards, Illuminadi’s expertise and professionalism are unmatched.
