Experience the enchantment of close-up magic in Nashville that defies belief. In this intimate setting, Illuminadi, the renowned magician and mentalist, mesmerizes audiences with sleight of hand artistry right before their eyes. Perfect for small gatherings, corporate booths, or private parties in Nashville, each 30-minute close-up magic performance by Illuminadi leaves up to 20 people utterly amazed, making it an unforgettable addition to any event in Music City.

Socially Distant

Parlour magic is compliant with CDC recommendations. Since the illusions are larger and more visually striking than close up magic, the audience can enjoy the magic from afar without feeling isolated.

Interactive

You don’t have to sit next to the magician to feel like a part of the show! The audience is involved and invested in the magic in their own seats, even when they’re spaced 6 feet apart!

Monthly

Illuminadi shows are as varied as the Nashville weather. Every month brings with it a fresh new show. Restore your wonder monthly with a different mind bending show.
