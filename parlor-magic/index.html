Parlor Magic

For those wishing to experience a cerebral and mind bending thrill

Capacity: Up to 40 people
Time: 40 minutes

Step into the world of parlor magic in Nashville, where wonder and mystery intertwine with captivating narratives.

Illuminadi, the sought-after magician and mentalist, takes center stage with a mesmerizing 40-minute performance that weaves emotional storylines, audience engagement, and astonishing feats of magic. Ideal for medium-sized corporate events and gatherings in Nashville, this immersive experience leaves up to 40 people spellbound, experiencing a journey they won’t soon forget. Elevate your corporate event or dinner party in Nashville with Illuminadi’s captivating parlor magic performance that will leave a lasting impression on your guests.

Socially Distant

Parlour magic is compliant with CDC recommendations. Since the illusions are larger and more visually striking than close up magic, the audience can enjoy the magic from afar without feeling isolated.

Interactive

You don’t have to sit next to the magician to feel like a part of the show! The audience is involved and invested in the magic in their own seats, even when they’re spaced 6 feet apart!

Monthly

Illuminadi shows are as varied as the Nashville weather. Every month brings with it a fresh new show. Restore your wonder monthly with a different mind bending show.
